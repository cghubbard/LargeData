Estadio Capitán San Luis is a multi-use stadium in Pinar del Río, Cuba. 
Inaugurated the 19 of January 1969, the stadium has a capacity of 8,000 
spectators. The stadium is mostly used for baseball games and is the home 
of the Pinar del Rio Baseball team. Previously, it was the home of Vegueros 
and Forestales baseball teams and Pinar del Rio, a football team. The football 
team's home field is now "La Bombonera" in San Critóbal. Coordinates: 22°25′25″N
