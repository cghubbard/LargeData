McFarland & Company, Inc. is a book publisher of primarily academic and 
adult nonfiction based in Jefferson, North Carolina. Its president and 
editor-in-chief is Robert Franklin, who founded the company in 1979.[1][2][3] 
McFarland employs a staff of about 50, and as of 2011 had published approximately 
5,000 titles.[4] Its target markets are libraries and direct mailing to 
enthusiasts in niche categories.[1] The company is known for its sports
