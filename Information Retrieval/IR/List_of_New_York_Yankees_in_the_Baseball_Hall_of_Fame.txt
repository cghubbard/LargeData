The New York Yankees are a Major League Baseball team based in The Bronx, 
New York. The team is in the East Division of the American League. Established 
in 1901 as the Baltimore Orioles, the team relocated to New York in 1903.

Hall of Fame and Museum, plus six broadcasters who have received the Hall's 
Ford C. Frick Award, spent part or all of their professional careers with 
the Yankees. The Frick Award, according to the Hall, "is presented annually
