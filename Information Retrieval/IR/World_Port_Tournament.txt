The World Port Tournament is an international baseball tournament held 
at the Neptunus Familiestadion in Rotterdam, Netherlands. The tournament 
has been held every other year (2005 excluded) in odd-numbered years since 
1985, alternating with the Haarlem Baseball Week. The tournament was created 
by Bram Peper, who was asked by Cuban Dr. Oscar Ferdinand Mell to organize 
an international baseball tournament, between national baseball teams all 
over the world, in Rotterdam, Netherlands. The 2015 World Port Tournament, 
the fifteenth edition of the event, was won by Cuba, a record seventh win.
