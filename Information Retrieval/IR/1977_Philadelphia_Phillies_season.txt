The 1977 Philadelphia Phillies season was the 95th season in the history 
of the franchise. The Phillies won their second consecutive National League 
East division title with a record of 101-61, five games over the Pittsburgh 
Pirates. The Phillies lost the NLCS to the Los Angeles Dodgers, three games 
to one. The Phillies were managed by Danny Ozark, as they played their
