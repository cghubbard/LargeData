Edward Burlton Heusser (May 7, 1909 – March 1, 1956) was a professional 
baseball player. He was a pitcher over parts of nine seasons (1935–1948) 
with the St. Louis Cardinals, Philadelphia Phillies, Philadelphia Athletics, 
and Cincinnati Reds. He was the National League ERA champion in 1944 with 
Cincinnati. For his career, he compiled a 56–67 record in 266 appearances 
with a 3.69 ERA and 299 strikeouts. While playing, Heusser earned the colorful
