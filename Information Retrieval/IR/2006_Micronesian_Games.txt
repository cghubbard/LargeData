The 6th Micronesian Games were held on Saipan, Northern Mariana Islands 
from June 23-July 2, 2006. The Games featured competition in 15 different 
sports/disciplines. In February 2006, the Games were canceled by the Governor 
of the Northern Marianas, Benigno R. Fitial, due to a financial crisis 
and a lack of planning. However, after pleas from athletes, coaches and 
parents, and the creation of a financial plan, the Games were allowed to 
go on. On June 23, 2006, the games were officially opened by the Governor 
of the Northern Mariana Islands, Benigno R. Fitial. Torch lighter was swimmer
