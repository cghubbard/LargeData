player. He was a pitcher over parts of 13 seasons (1960–1972) with the 
Pittsburgh Pirates, San Francisco Giants, Cincinnati Reds and Houston Astros. 
Gibbon was a member of the 1960 World Series champion Pirates. An alumnus
