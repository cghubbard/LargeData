The following are the baseball events of the year 1994 throughout the world. 
As a result of a players' strike, the MLB season ended prematurely on August 
11, 1994. No postseason (including the World Series) was played. Minor 
League Baseball was not affected. During the shortened Major League Baseball 
season, the league adorned uniforms and stadiums to announce the 125th 
anniversary of baseball's first professional team, the Cincinnati Red Stockings.
