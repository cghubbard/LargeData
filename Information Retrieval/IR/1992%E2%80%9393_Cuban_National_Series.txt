The 32nd Cuban National Series saw a slight contraction of the league. 
The successful Vegueros team, from Pinar del Río Province combined with 
its pinareño rival, Forestales, into a new team called Pinar del Río. Further, 
Henequeneros and Citricultores, both of which had been successful in earlier 
series, merged to become Matanzas. For the first time the teams were split 
into 4 groups rather than 2. The newly formed Pinar del Río team lost a
