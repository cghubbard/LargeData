The New York Collegiate Baseball League (NYCBL) is a collegiate summer 
baseball league founded in 1978 and sanctioned by the National Alliance 
of College Summer Baseball, National Amateur Baseball Federation and Major 
League Baseball. Each NYCBL team plays a 46- game schedule from June to 
July with a playoff in early August. Several players from this league have 
become Major Leaguers. For the 2014 season, the league has 13 teams located 
in central and western New York.[1] There are 13 teams in an Eastern and 
Western division scheduled to play during the 2014 season. This season
