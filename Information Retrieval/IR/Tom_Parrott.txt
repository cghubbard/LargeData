Thomas William Parrott [Tacky Tom] (April 10, 1868 – January 1, 1932) was 
a professional baseball player. He was a right-handed pitcher over parts 
of four seasons (1893–1896) with the Chicago Colts, Cincinnati Reds and 
St. Louis Browns. For his career, he compiled a 39–48 record in 115 appearances, 
with an 5.33 earned run average and 166 strikeouts. Parrott was born in
