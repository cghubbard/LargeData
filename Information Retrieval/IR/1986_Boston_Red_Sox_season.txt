The 1986 Boston Red Sox season involved the Red Sox finishing 1st in the 
American League East with a record of 95 wins and 66 losses. The Boston 
Red Sox held spring training at Chain of Lakes Park in Winter Haven, Florida 
for the 21st season. 1986 was the first time since 1904 that the Yankees 
finished second in the standings to the Red Sox.[6] [1] [2] [3] [4] [5]
