the Major League Baseball All-Star Game. The game will be hosted by the 
Miami Marlins and will be played at Marlins Park on July 11, 2017. It will 
be televised nationally on Fox. The winner will have home field advantage 
for the 2017 World Series. The host city was announced on February 10, 
2015 by MLB Commissioner Rob Manfred. This will be the first time ever
