The Cincinnati Reds' 2009 season was a season in American baseball. It 
involved the Cincinnati Reds attempting to win the NL Central after finishing 
fifth in the division the previous year.[1] For the second year, the Reds 
were managed by Dusty Baker.[1][2] The Reds played their seventh season 
of home games in Great American Ball Park. The Reds finished the 2009 season
