The 1955 Major League Baseball season was contested from April to October 
1955. It featured 16 teams, eight in the National League and eight in the 
American League, with each team playing a 154-game schedule. In the World 
Series the Brooklyn Dodgers defeated the New York Yankees 4 games to 3.
