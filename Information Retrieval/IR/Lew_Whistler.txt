Lewis W. Whistler, born as Lewis Wissler, (March 10, 1868 – December 30, 
1959), was an American Major League Baseball player who played the majority 
of his career as a first baseman. In his four-season career, he played 
for the New York Giants (1890–1891), Baltimore Orioles (1892), Louisville 
Colonels (1893), and St. Louis Browns. His major league totals include: 
272 games played, 1014 at bats, and a .244 batting average. Whistler died
