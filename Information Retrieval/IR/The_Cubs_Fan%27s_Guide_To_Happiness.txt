Ellis. It deals with the different views of Chicago Cubs fans. It talks 
about things such as the theory of "There's Always Next Year" and other 
philosophies that Cubs fans live by. Ellis uses the thoughts of historic 
philosophers to prove some of the thought processes of Cubs fans that he
