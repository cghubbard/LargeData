The 1978 Kansas City Royals season was a season in American baseball. It 
involved the Royals finishing first in the American League West with a 
record of 92 wins and 70 losses. The team went on to lose in the 1978 American 
League Championship Series to the New York Yankees, 3 games to 1. [1] [2]
