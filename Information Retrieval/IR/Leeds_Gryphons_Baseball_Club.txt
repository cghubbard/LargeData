Leeds Gryphons Baseball Club, also known as Leeds University Union Baseball 
Club, was founded in December 2014 by Christopher Bowen, Jacob Davies and 
Matthew Jackson at the University of Leeds.[1] The team competes in the 
Northern University baseball league. This is the second baseball club at 
the University of Leeds after the 2009-2011 Leeds Oaks University Baseball 
Club, which was a member of the British University Baseball Association.[2]
