John Edward Barbato (born July 11, 1992) is an American professional baseball 
pitcher for the New York Yankees of Major League Baseball (MLB). Barbato 
was drafted by the San Diego Padres in the sixth round of the 2010 Major 
League Baseball draft out of Felix Varela High School in Miami, Florida. 
The Padres traded Barbato to the New York Yankees for Shawn Kelley on December 
29, 2014.[1] The Yankees added him to their 40-man roster after the 2015 
season.[2] He made his major league debut on April 5, 2016. He was optioned 
to Triple-A on May 9, 2016, recalled on August 4th, and sent back again 
on August 6. In 13 games with the Yankees in 2016, Barbato had a 1-2 record
