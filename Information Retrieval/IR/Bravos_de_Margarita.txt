Home Away The Bravos de Margarita (Margarita Braves) are a professional 
baseball team who plays in the Venezuelan Professional Baseball League 
since the 2007-08 season. The team was founded in 1991 as the Petroleros 
de Cabimas (Cabimas Oilers), and played through the 1994-95 season. Later 
was renamed as Pastora de Occidente (Shepherds of the West) from 1995-96 
to 1996-97, being also known as Pastora de los Llanos (Shepherds of the 
Plains) from 1997-98 to 2006-07. Since their inception, the Bravos de Margarita 
have played at Estadio Nueva Esparta, which is located in Margarita Island.
