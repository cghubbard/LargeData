former Major League Baseball catcher. Signed by the St. Louis Cardinals 
as an amateur free agent in 1974, Brummer made his Major League Baseball 
debut with the St. Louis Cardinals on May 25, 1981, and appeared in his 
final major league game on October 6, 1985. Brummer was a member of the 
St. Louis Cardinals team that defeated the Milwaukee Brewers in the 1982
