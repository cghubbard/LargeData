The following is a list of Major League Baseball players who played at 
least ten full seasons and spent their entire MLB playing careers exclusively 
with one team (some of those listed subsequently went on to coach or manage 
with other teams). As of the end of the 2015 Major League season, 168 players 
have accomplished this feat, of which the New York Yankees have had the 
most, with 25. Derek Jeter of the Yankees is the most recent player to 
complete a full career with one team. Researched through Baseball Reference
