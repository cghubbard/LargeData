John Ernest Miller (born May 30, 1941) is an American former professional 
baseball player. He played in all or part of five seasons in Major League 
Baseball for the Baltimore Orioles between 1962 through 1967. Miller was 
originally signed as an amateur free agent prior to the 1961 season by 
the Baltimore Orioles. He was part of the 1966 World Series championship 
team, although he did not appear in the postseason. On May 10, 1967, his 
contract was purchased from the Orioles by the New York Mets,[1] but he 
never appeared in a major league game for them, spending the remainder
