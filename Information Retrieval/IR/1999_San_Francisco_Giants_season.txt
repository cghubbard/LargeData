The 1999 San Francisco Giants season was the Giants' 117th season in Major 
League Baseball, their 42nd season in San Francisco since their move from 
New York following the 1957 season, and their 40th and final season at 
3Com Park at Candlestick Point. The team finished in second place in the 
National League West with an 86-76 record, 14 games behind the Arizona
