Sangdomg Baseball Stadium is a baseball stadium in Gimhae, South Korea. 
The land area totals 135,000 square meters, and the stadium itself was 
built with over 25 billion won of capital. The ultramodern facilities include 
the main stadium, the game management hall, the Giants Hall, and the Giants 
Dome. The left and right fences are 97 m away from the plate, and the center 
fence 120 m away. The stadium complex is currently utilized as the home 
field of the Lotte Giants in the Korea Baseball Futures League. Coordinates:
