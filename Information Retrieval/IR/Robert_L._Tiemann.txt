Robert L. Tiemann is an American baseball historian based in St. Louis, 
and a member of SABR (the Society of American Baseball Research).[1][2][3][4][5][6] 
He is considered a 19th-century baseball expert.[7][8] Tiemann has written 
or edited several baseball books, including Nineteenth Century Stars, Baseball's 
First Stars, Cardinal Classics: Outstanding Games from Each of the St. 
Louis Baseball Clubs 100 Seasons and The National Pastime: A Review of
