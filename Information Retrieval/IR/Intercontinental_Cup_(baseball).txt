The Intercontinental Cup was a baseball tournament between the members 
of the then-International Baseball Federation (IBAF). It was first held 
in 1973 in Italy, and was held every other year following until 1999. After 
1999, the tournament was held every few four years -- in 2002, 2006 and 
2010. As with many international baseball competitions, it was dominated 
by Cuba which won 10 gold and 3 silver in 16 tournaments. The most recent 
tournament was held in 2010, in Taichung, Taiwan, and appears to have been 
the last time the tournament was held.[1][2] The 1973 and 1975 tourneys
