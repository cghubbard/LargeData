The Quinnipiac Bobcats are the nineteen sports teams representing Quinnipiac 
University in Hamden, Connecticut in intercollegiate athletics, including 
men and women's basketball, cross country, hockey, lacrosse, soccer, tennis, 
and track and field; women's-only field hockey, golf, softball, and volleyball; 
and men's-only baseball. The Bobcats compete in the NCAA Division I and 
are members of the Metro Atlantic Athletic Conference, joining on July 
1, 2013 after being in the Northeast Conference.[2] The Bobcats compete 
outside the MAAC in three sports. Despite the MAAC sponsoring field hockey,
