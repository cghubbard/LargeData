Gorilla Gold Grip Enhancer is a towel used by athletes to improve their 
grip on sports equipment. The product releases a wax-based substance that 
repels moisture. It is effective in sports that require good grips, such 
as American football, baseball, golf, tennis, hockey, and weightlifting.[1][2][3]

and minor league games as an alternative to pine tar.[2] In 2012, National
