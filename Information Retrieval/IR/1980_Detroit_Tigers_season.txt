The 1980 Detroit Tigers finished in fourth place in the American League 
East with a record of 84-78, 19 games behind the Yankees. They outscored 
their opponents 830 to 757. The Tigers drew 1,785,293 fans to Tiger Stadium 
in 1980, ranking 7th of the 14 teams in the American League. [1] [2] [3]
