William Mitchell (December 1, 1889 – November 23, 1973) born in Pleasant 
Grove, Mississippi,[1] was a pitcher for the Cleveland Naps/Indians (1909-1916) 
and Detroit Tigers (1916-1919). In 11 seasons, he had an 84–92 record in 
276 games pitched with 93 complete games, 16 shutouts, 4 saves, 1632 innings 
pitched, 605 walks allowed, 921 strikeouts, 75 hit batsmen, 48 wild pitches
