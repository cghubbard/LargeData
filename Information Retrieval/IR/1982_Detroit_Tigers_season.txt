The 1982 Detroit Tigers finished in fourth place in the American League 
East with a record of 83-79 (.512), 12 games behind the Brewers. The Tigers 
outscored their opponents 729 to 685. The Tigers drew 1,636,058 fans to 
Tiger Stadium in 1982, ranking 7th of the 14 teams in the American League.
