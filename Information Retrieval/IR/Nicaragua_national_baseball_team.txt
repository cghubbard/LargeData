The Nicaragua national baseball team is the national baseball team of Nicaragua. 
Considered the best in Central America along with Panama. It represents 
Nicaragua in international tournaments.Nicaragua has a long history in 
the Baseball World Cup, hosting it five times and earning five second-place 
finishes the last in 1990. A group of 30 Nicaraguans signed to professional 
contracts in the United States of America. Dennis Martínez, Tony Chévez,
