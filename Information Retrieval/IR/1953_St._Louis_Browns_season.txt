The 1953 St. Louis Browns season was a season in American baseball. It 
involved the Browns finishing 8th in the American League with a record 
of 54 wins and 100 losses, 46½ games behind the AL and World Series champion 
New York Yankees in their 52nd and final season in the Gateway City. After 
the season, the Browns moved to Baltimore, where they play today, and became
