Nguyen Van Troi Stadium is a multi-use stadium in Guantánamo, Cuba. It 
is currently used mostly for baseball games and is the home stadium of 
Guantánamo Indios. The stadium holds 14,000 people. The stadium is named 
after the Vietnamese communist guerrilla fighter, Nguyen Van Troi, who 
was executed after trying to assassinate US Secretary of State Robert S. 
McNamara during a visit to South Vietnam. Coordinates: 20°8′12″N 75°11′52″W﻿
