The 1980 San Francisco Giants season was the Giants' 98th season in Major 
League Baseball, their 23rd season in San Francisco since their move from 
New York following the 1957 season, and their 21st at Candlestick Park. 
The team finished in fifth place in the National League West with an 75-86
