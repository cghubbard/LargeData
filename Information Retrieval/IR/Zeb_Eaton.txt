Zebulon Vance Eaton (February 2, 1920 – December 17, 1989), nicknamed "Red," 
was a right-handed pitcher in Major League Baseball with the Detroit Tigers 
in 1944 and 1945. Born in Cooleemee, North Carolina, Zeb Eaton played in 
6 games for the Tigers in 1944 with no decisions and a 5.74 earned run 
average. In 1945, he played in 17 games and had a record of 4–2 with an 
improved ERA of 4.05. Eaton also contributed as a hitter in 1945, with 
2 home runs, 10 RBIs, and a double in 32 at bats. Eaton's last major league
