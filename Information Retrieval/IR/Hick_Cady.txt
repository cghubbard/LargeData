Forrest Leroy "Hick" Cady (January 26, 1886 – March 3, 1946) was a backup 
catcher in Major League Baseball who played for the Boston Red Sox (1912–17) 
and Philadelphia Phillies (1919). Cady batted and threw right-handed. He 
was born in Bishop Hill, Illinois. In a seven-season career, Cady was a
