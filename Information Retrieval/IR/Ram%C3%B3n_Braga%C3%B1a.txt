Ramón "El Profesor" Bragaña (May 11, 1909 - May 11, 1985) was an Cuban 
baseball pitcher and outfielder in the Negro leagues and the Mexican League. 
He played professionally from 1928 to 1955, mostly with the Cuban Stars 
(East) (1928-1930), the Azules de Veracruz (1940-1951), and the Águila 
de Veracruz (1955).[1][2] He was elected to the Cuban Baseball Hall of
