The 1955 Cincinnati Redlegs season was a season in American baseball. It 
consisted of the Redlegs finishing in fifth place in the National League, 
with a record of 75–79, 23½ games behind the NL and World Series Champion 
Brooklyn Dodgers. The Redlegs were managed by Birdie Tebbetts and played
