Sylvester W. Johnson, ne Sylvester Johnson[1] (December 31, 1900 – February 
20, 1985), was an American Major League Baseball pitcher. His career lasted 
from 1922–1940 and he played for the St. Louis Cardinals, Cincinnati Reds, 
Detroit Tigers, and Philadelphia Phillies. He was inducted into the Oregon 
Sports Hall of Fame in 1981. He was a coach for the Phillies from 1937–1941.
