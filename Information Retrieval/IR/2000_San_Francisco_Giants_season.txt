The 2000 San Francisco Giants season was the Giants' 118th season in Major 
League Baseball and their 43rd season in San Francisco since their move 
from New York following the 1957 season. The Giants finished in first place 
in the National League West with a record of 97 wins and 65 losses. They 
lost the National League Division Series in four games to the New York 
Mets. The team played their first season in newly opened Pacific Bell Park.
