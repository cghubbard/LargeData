Frank Henry O'Connor (September 15, 1868 – December 26, 1913) was an American 
baseball pitcher who played for the Philadelphia Phillies in 1893. He attended 
Dartmouth College. O'Connor was born on September 15, 1868, in Keeseville, 
New York. He was 24 years old when he broke into the big leagues on August 
3, 1893, with the Philadelphia Phillies as a left-handed pitcher. He played 
in only three games with a win–loss record of 0–0 and an earned run average
