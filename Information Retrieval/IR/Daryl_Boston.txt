being drafted by the Chicago White Sox in the first round (seventh overall) 
of the 1981 Major League Baseball Draft, Boston made his MLB debut with 
the White Sox on May 13, 1984, and appeared in his final game on August 
11, 1994. On October 4, 1986, he homered off Bert Blyleven. This was the

most homers allowed in one year. The New York Mets selected Boston off
