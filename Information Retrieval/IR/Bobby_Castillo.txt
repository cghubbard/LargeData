Robert Ernie "Babo"[1] Castillo Jr. (April 18, 1955 – June 30, 2014) was 
a pitcher in Major League Baseball. He played for the Los Angeles Dodgers 
and Minnesota Twins between 1977 and 1985. He was in the bullpen for the 
Dodgers in the 1981 World Series, pitching one inning against the New York 
Yankees. He also pitched one season in Japan for the Chunichi Dragons in 
1987. Castillo is credited with teaching his former Dodgers team-mate,
