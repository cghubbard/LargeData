"In no sport are nicknames more pervasive than baseball."[1] This is a 
list of nicknames of Major League Baseball teams and players. It includes 
a complete list of nicknames of players in the Baseball Hall of Fame, a 
list of nicknames of current players, nicknames of popular players who 
have played for each major league team, and lists of nicknames grouped 
into particular categories (e.g., ethnic nicknames, personality trait nicknames 
etc.). It also includes a list of nicknames of current Major League teams.
