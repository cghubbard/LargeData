The Gulf Coast League (GCL) Phillies are a minor league baseball located 
at the Carpenter Complex, in Clearwater, Florida. A member of the rookie-level 
Gulf Coast League, the Phillies are an affiliate of the Philadelphia Phillies. 
The team is composed mainly of players who are in their first year of professional 
baseball as either draftees or non-drafted free agents from the United 
States, Canada, Dominican Republic, Venezuela, and various other countries. 
The GCL Phillies played some games at the Joe DiMaggio Complex in Clearwater 
in 2009 while the Carpenter Complex was being renovated.[1] The GCL Phillies
