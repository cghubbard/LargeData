Beaver Dam is a home rule-class city[1] in Ohio County, Kentucky, in the 
United States. The population was 3,515 at the 2010 census. It is named 
for the Beaver Dam Baptist Church which predates the town by 65 years.[citation 
needed] The city was formally incorporated by the state assembly in 1873.[2] 
Beaver Dam is located at 37°24′26″N 86°52′40″W﻿ / ﻿37.40722°N 86.87778°W﻿
