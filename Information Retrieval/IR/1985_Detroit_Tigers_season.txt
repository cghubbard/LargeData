Eastern Division with a record of 84-77 (.522), 15 games behind the Blue 
Jays. The Tigers outscored their opponents 729 to 688. The Tigers drew 
2,286,609 fans to Tiger Stadium in 1983, ranking 3rd of the 14 teams in
