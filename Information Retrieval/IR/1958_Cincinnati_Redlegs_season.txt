The 1958 Cincinnati Redlegs season consisted of the Redlegs finishing in 
fourth place in the National League standings with a record of 76–78, 16 
games behind the Milwaukee Braves. The Redlegs played their home games 
at Crosley Field. The season started with Birdie Tebbetts managing the 
club, but after the Redlegs went 52–61, Tebbetts was replaced in August 
by Jimmy Dykes, who went 24–17 the rest of the way. Infielders Coaches
