Baseball is not a mandatory sport in the Summer Universiade. In 1993, the 
sport was held at the XVII Summer Universiade in Buffalo, USA, and it was 
staged two years after at the XVIII Summer Universiade in Fukuoka, Japan. 
Twenty years later, the sport made a comeback at the 2015 Summer Universiade 
in Gwangju, South Korea and will played again at the 2017 Summer Universiade
