The LIU Brooklyn Blackbirds refer to the 16 sports teams representing Long 
Island University's campus in Brooklyn, New York in intercollegiate athletics, 
including men and women's basketball, cross country, golf, soccer, and 
track; women's-only bowling, lacrosse, softball, tennis, and volleyball; 
and men's-only baseball. The Blackbirds compete in the NCAA Division I
