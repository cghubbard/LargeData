The Junior League Softball World Series is a softball tournament for girls 
aged between 12 and 14.[1] The tournament is administered by Little League 
Baseball, Inc. and is held annually in the Seattle suburb of Kirkland, 
Washington. The tournament started in 1999, and is a true "World Series" 
since ten regions from around the world send in teams: The ten teams are 
divided into two pools of five members each. The two best teams from each 
pool then advance to the semi-finals. The winners in the semi-finals play
