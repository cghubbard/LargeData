Christy Mathewson–Memorial Stadium is a 13,100-seat multi-purpose stadium 
at Bucknell University in Lewisburg, Pennsylvania. Originally built in 
1924, the stadium was renovated and renamed in honor of Mathewson in 1989. 
It is home to the Bucknell Bison football team and the Lewisburg Area High 
School Green Dragons football team. It is named for Christy Mathewson, 
a Bucknell alumnus who went on to become a Hall of Fame pitcher for the 
New York Giants in the early 20th century. Mathewson was on the Walter 
Camp All-American football team as a kicker while a student at Bucknell.
