The 1981 San Francisco Giants season was the Giants' 99th season in Major 
League Baseball, their 24th season in San Francisco since their move from 
New York following the 1957 season, and their 22nd at Candlestick Park. 
Giants manager Frank Robinson became the first black manager in the history 
of the National League.[1] Robinson was also the first black manager in
