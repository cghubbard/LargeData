In 1971, the Baltimore Orioles finished first in the American League East, 
with a record of 101 wins and 57 losses. As of 2016, the 1971 Orioles are 
one of only two Major League Baseball clubs (the 1920 Chicago White Sox 
being the other) to have four 20-game winners in a season: Jim Palmer, 
Dave McNally, Mike Cuellar, and Pat Dobson.[1] [1] [2] [3] [4] [5] [6]
