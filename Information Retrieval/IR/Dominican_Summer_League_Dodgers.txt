The Dominican Summer Dodgers are a minor league baseball team in the Dominican 
Summer League. The team plays in the Boca Chica Northwest division and 
is affiliated with the Los Angeles Dodgers. They have operated since 1989. 
Occasionally the Dodgers have fielded two teams in the DSL. In 1990, they 
had the DSL Cibao Dodgers and the DSL San Pedro de Macoris Dodgers and 
from 1992-2004 they had the DSL Dodgers 1 and DSL Dodgers 2. Pitchers Catchers
