The 2015 San Francisco Giants season was the Giants' 133rd year in Major 
League Baseball, their 58th year in San Francisco since their move from 
New York following the 1957 season, and their 16th at AT&T Park. The team 
entered the season as the defending World Series champions and got second 
place in the NL West for the second straight year, but missed the playoffs 
with a record of 84-78 (.519). All schedule and scores taken from MLB.com.[34]
