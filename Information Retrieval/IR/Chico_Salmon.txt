17, 2000 in Bocas del Toro, Panama) was a Panamanian Major League Baseball 
utility player. He played for the Cleveland Indians (1964–1968) and the 
Baltimore Orioles (1969–1972), where he appeared in the World Series in 
1969, 1970, and 1971. Salmon was selected by the Seattle Pilots in the
