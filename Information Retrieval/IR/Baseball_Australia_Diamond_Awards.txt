The Baseball Australia Diamond Awards — begun in 2006[1] — are given annually 
by the Australian Baseball Federation, to honor persons who have excelled 
in playing, managing, or serving the sport of baseball. The awards ceremony 
is usually held in or about February, soon after the conclusion of that 
year's season, which starts in or about November of the preceding year.
