sports writer who covered the St. Louis Cardinals from 1915 to 1958. He 
was hired by the St. Louis Post-Dispatch in 1918, working there for the 
majority of his career. He also covered the St. Louis Terriers of the Federal 
League in 1915, served as president of the Florida State League, and was
