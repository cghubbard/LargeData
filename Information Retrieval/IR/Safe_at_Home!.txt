Safe at Home! is a 1962 sports comedy film starring Major League Baseball 
players Mickey Mantle and Roger Maris of the New York Yankees.[1] The film 
also stars William Frawley and Don Collier, with appearances by New York 
Yankees Whitey Ford and Ralph Houk. The film is about a young Florida boy 
who lies to his Little League teammates, telling them he knows Mantle and 
Maris and will bring them to their team banquet. His attempts to meet his 
heroes during spring training by sneaking into the stadium and into their 
hotel room at the Yankee Clipper Hotel result in chaos, and he learns an
