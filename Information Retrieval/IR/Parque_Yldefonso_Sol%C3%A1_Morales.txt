Parque Yldefonso Solá Morales is a multi-use stadium in Caguas, Puerto 
Rico. It is currently used mostly for baseball games and is the home of 
Criollos de Caguas. The stadium holds 10,000 people. The hockey events 
for the 2010 Central American and Caribbean Games were held there.[1] Coordinates:
