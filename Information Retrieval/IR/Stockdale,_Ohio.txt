Stockdale is an unincorporated community in southern Marion Township, Pike 
County, Ohio, United States. Although it is unincorporated, it has a post 
office, with the ZIP code of 45683.[1] Stockdale is served by the Minford 
Telephone Company and Eastern Local Schools. Baseball Hall of Famer Branch 
Rickey was born in Stockdale in 1881. Stockdale community sign. Looking 
east on Ohio Highway 335 in Stockdale. Coordinates: 38°57′23″N 82°51′30″W﻿
