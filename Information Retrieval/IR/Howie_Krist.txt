Howard Wilbur Krist (February 28, 1916 – April 23, 1989) was a pitcher 
in Major League Baseball from 1937 to 1946. "Spud" played his entire career 
for the St. Louis Cardinals of the National League, and was a member of 
the 1942 and 1946 World Series Championship teams. Krist's 10–0 record 
in 1941, his first full season, is the third-best undefeated season ever.
