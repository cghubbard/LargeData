DeNA Co., Ltd. (株式会社ディー・エヌ・エー, Kabushikigaisha Dī-Enu-Ē?) (pronounced "DNA") 
is a provider of mobile portal and e-commerce websites based in Japan. 
It owns the Mobage platform, [2] which is one of the most popular cell 
phone gaming platforms in Japan. It also operates many other services, 
including a popular e-commerce website DeNA Shopping (formerly: Bidders).
