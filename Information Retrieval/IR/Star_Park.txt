Star Park is a former baseball ground located at Taylor and Salina Streets 
in Syracuse, New York, United States.[1] The ground was home to the Syracuse 
Stars from 1885 to 1899 and from 1902 to 1904, and its minor league predecessors 
and successors until 1929. It was also the home of the Syracuse Pros football 
team, who were possible members of the American Professional Football Association 
(later renamed the National Football League), in 1921. Coordinates: 43°02′22″N
