Henry J. "Handsome Harry" Boyle (September 20, 1860 – May 25, 1932) was 
a professional baseball player. He was a pitcher over parts of 6 seasons 
(1884–1889) with the St. Louis Maroons and Indianapolis Hoosiers. He led 
the National League in ERA in 1886 while playing for St. Louis. For his 
career, he compiled an 89–111 record in 207 appearances, with a 3.06 ERA
