Hoosiers. The first Western League, which lasted only part of the 1885 
season, included an Indianapolis Hoosiers team. Another Hoosiers minor-league 
team came into existence in 1894. It was a charter member of the third 
Western League, which became the American League in 1900. This team was 
contracted when the AL became an official major league in 1901. One of
