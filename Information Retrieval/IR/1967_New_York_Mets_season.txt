The 1967 New York Mets season was the 6th regular season for the Mets. 
They went 61–101 and finished 10th in the National League, 40½ games behind 
the NL pennant and World Series Champion St. Louis Cardinals. They were 
managed by Wes Westrum and Salty Parker. They played home games at Shea
