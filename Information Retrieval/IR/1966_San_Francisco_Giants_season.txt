The 1966 San Francisco Giants season was the Giants' 84th year in Major 
League Baseball, their ninth year in San Francisco since their move from 
New York following the 1957 season, and their seventh at Candlestick Park. 
The Giants finished second in the National League with a record of 93 wins 
and 68 losses, a game-and-a-half behind their arch-rivals, the NL champion
