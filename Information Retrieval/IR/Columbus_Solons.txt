The Columbus Solons were a baseball team in the American Association from 
1889 to 1891. In three seasons, they won 200 games and lost 209 for a winning 
percentage of .489. Their home games were played at Recreation Park in 
Columbus, Ohio. The Solons were managed by Al Buckenberger (99-119), Gus 
Schmelz (99-89), and Pat Sullivan (2-1). Some of their top players were
