Henry Harrison "Hi" Myers (April 27, 1889 – May 1, 1965) was a professional 
baseball player. He was an outfielder over all or part of 14 seasons (1909–1925) 
with the Brooklyn Supurbas/Robins, St. Louis Cardinals, and Cincinnati 
Reds. He led the National League in RBIs in 1919 while playing for Brooklyn.
