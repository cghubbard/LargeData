The New York Yankees' 1993 season was the 91st season for the Yankees. 
The team finished with a record of 88-74 finishing 7 games behind the Toronto 
Blue Jays. New York was managed by Buck Showalter. The Yankees played at 
Yankee Stadium. With the exceptions of 2008, 2013 and 2014, the Yankees 
have made the playoffs in every non-strike season since. [1] [2] [3] [4]
