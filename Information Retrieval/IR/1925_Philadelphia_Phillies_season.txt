The 1925 Philadelphia Phillies season was a season in Major League Baseball. 
The Phillies finished seventh in the National League with a record of 68 
wins and 85 losses. The Phillies trained in Bradenton, Florida in 1925. 
Shortly after beginning camp, catcher Butch Henline was named team captain.[1]
