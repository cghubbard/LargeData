Kazuhiro Yamauchi (山内 一弘, Yamauchi Kazuhiro?, May 1, 1932 – February 2, 
2009) was a Japanese baseball player and manager. He played for the Mainichi 
Orions, the Hanshin Tigers and the Hiroshima Toyo Carp over the span of 
an 18 season-long career (1952–1970). Some of his stats include 7702 at 
bats, 1218 runs, 2271 hits, 396 home runs, 1286 runs batted in, 118 stolen 
bases, 1061 walks, and a batting average of .292.[1] He was inducted into 
the Japanese Baseball Hall of Fame in 2002.[2] Yamauchi died of liver failure
