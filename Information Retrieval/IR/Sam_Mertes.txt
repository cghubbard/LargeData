Samuel Blair Mertes (August 6, 1872 – March 12, 1945) was a professional 
baseball player. He was an outfielder over parts of 10 seasons (1896–1906) 
with the Philadelphia Phillies, Chicago Orphans, Chicago White Sox, New 
York Giants, and St. Louis Cardinals. He led the National League in RBIs
