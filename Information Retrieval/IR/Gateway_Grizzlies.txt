The Gateway Grizzlies are a professional baseball team based in the St. 
Louis suburb of Sauget, Illinois, in the United States. The Grizzlies are 
a member of the West Division of the Frontier League, which is not affiliated 
with Major League Baseball. The Grizzlies played their home games during 
their inaugural 2001 season at Sauget Field.[1] In 2002, GCS Ballpark (formerly 
GMC Stadium) opened and has remained the club's home since. Pitchers Utility
