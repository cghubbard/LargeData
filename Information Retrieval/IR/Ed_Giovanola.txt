Edward Thomas Giovanola (born March 4, 1969 in Los Gatos, California) is 
a former Major League Baseball infielder. He is an alumnus of Bellarmine 
College Preparatory and Santa Clara University. Drafted by the Atlanta 
Braves in the 7th round of the 1990 MLB amateur draft, Giovanola spent 
several years with Atlanta's Double-A minor league affiliate, the Greenville 
Braves, before becoming a standout player with the Triple-A Richmond Braves, 
culminating in his Major League Baseball debut with the Atlanta Braves
