The Syracuse Stars was the name of several Minor league baseball teams 
who played between 1877 and 1929. The Stars were based in Syracuse, New 
York, and played in the International League, affiliated with the League 
Alliance; the New York State League (1885, 1902-1917), Eastern Association 
(1891), Eastern League (1892, 1894-1901), International League (1886-1887), 
International Association (1888-1889), and New York-Pennsylvania League
