Aris Baseball Club is a baseball club based in Thessaloniki, Greece, and 
is owned by one of the biggest sports clubs in Greece, Aris Thessaloniki. 
The club was established in 2002 and is the only major baseball club in 
Macedonia and one of the most important in Greece. From its first participation 
in the Hellenic Baseball League, Aris managed to finish in the first three 
places, being able to play in three international league cups. Also Aris
