The Bristol Pirates are a minor league baseball team in Bristol, Virginia, 
USA. They are a Rookie-level team in the Appalachian League. As of October 
16, 2013, they are owned by the Pittsburgh Pirates.[1] The team plays home 
games at DeVault Memorial Stadium. Opened in 1969, Devault Memorial Stadium 
seats 2,000 fans. The team was previously affiliated with the Detroit Tigers, 
and a previous manager was retired Tigers manager Jim Leyland. They were 
a farm team of the Chicago White Sox from 1995 to 2013 as the Bristol White 
Sox. The team is operated by a non-profit organization, Bristol Baseball, 
Incorporated (BBI). BBI has no full-time paid staff, instead relying on 
a volunteer board and General Manager to keep and promote professional
