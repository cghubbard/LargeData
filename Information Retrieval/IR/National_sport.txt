A national sport or national pastime is a sport or game that is considered 
to be an intrinsic part of the culture of a nation. Some sports are de 
facto (not established by law) national sports, as baseball is in the United 
States and cricket is in England, while others are de jure (established 
by law) national sports, as lacrosse and ice hockey are in Canada. These 
sports do not have to be necessarily the most played or most followed,
