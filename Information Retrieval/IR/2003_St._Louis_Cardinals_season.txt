The St. Louis Cardinals 2003 season was the team's 122nd season in St. 
Louis, Missouri and the 112th season in the National League. The Cardinals 
went 85-77 during the season and finished 3rd in the National League Central 
division, three games behind the Chicago Cubs, who won the NL Central at 
88-74, and two behind the NL Central runners-up, the Houston Astros (87-75).
