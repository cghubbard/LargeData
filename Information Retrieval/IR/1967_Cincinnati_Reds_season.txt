The 1967 Cincinnati Reds season consisted of the Reds finishing in fourth 
place in the National League with a record of 87–75, 14½ games behind the 
NL and World Series Champion St. Louis Cardinals. The Reds were managed 
by Dave Bristol and played their home games at Crosley Field. On August
