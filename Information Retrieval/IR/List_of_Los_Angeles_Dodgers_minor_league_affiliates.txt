List of all Los Angeles Dodgers Minor League Affiliates in the team's history. 
This list compiles the history of their minor league affiliates since the 
reorganization of the Minor League classification season prior to the 1963 
season. Organized baseball reclassified its minor league system prior to 
the 1963 season. The classification of leagues as B, C, D, and E changed 
to the current A-based rankings: AAA, AA, A, short-season A, and Rookie-Advanced. 
Many leagues were reclassified. Class D day would be equivalent to the 
Rookie level today. The Eastern League and Southern League moved up to 
AA; the Carolina League changed from B to A; the Northern League and California 
League moved from C to A; the Florida State League, Midwest League, Western 
Carolinas League (later renamed the South Atlantic League), and the Georgia–Florida 
League all moved from D to A. The New York–Penn League became an A (short-season) 
league, having previously been a D League. The other short-season A league,
