Estadio 5 de Septiembre (Spanish: Fifth of September) is a multi-use stadium 
in Cienfuegos, Cuba. It is used mostly for baseball games and is the home 
stadium of Cienfuegos Camaroneros. The stadium holds 15,600 people.[1]
