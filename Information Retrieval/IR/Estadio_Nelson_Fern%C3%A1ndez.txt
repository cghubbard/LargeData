Estadio Nelson Fernández is a multi-use stadium in San José de las Lajas, 
Mayabeque Province, Cuba. It is currently used mostly for baseball games 
and up to 2011 was the home stadium of the Havana Vaqueros team. Starting 
from the 2011-2012 series this stadium will be the home of the new Mayabeque 
provincial team "Huracanes" (hurricanes). The stadium holds 8,000 people.
