The Australian National Youth Championships is a series of annual youth 
events run by the Australian Baseball Federation. There are tournaments 
for U18 (AAA), U16 (AA) and U14 (A) levels and are competed by Queensland, 
New South Wales, Victoria, South Australia, Western Australia, Territories 
(ACT & North Territory), New South Wales Country and Victoria Provincial. 
Teams are picked by the state governing bodies from their respective state 
titles. The tournaments also serve a purpose for national teams to be picked.
