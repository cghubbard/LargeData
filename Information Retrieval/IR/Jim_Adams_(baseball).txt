James J. "Jim" Adams (born 1868) was an American major league baseball 
catcher. He played professionally for the St. Louis Browns. Adams was born 
in 1868 in East St. Louis, Illinois.[1] He played in one game for the St. 
Louis Browns on April 21, 1890.[2] He hit one single in four at-bats during 
the game. In addition to his brief appearance for the Browns, he played 
on various minor league teams from 1889–1892 and again in 1899–1900. He
