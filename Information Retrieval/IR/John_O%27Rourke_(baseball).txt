John W. O'Rourke (August 23, 1849 – June 23, 1911) was a 19th-century baseball 
player. Between 1879 and 1883, he played in the National League with the 
Boston Red Caps (1879–1880) and in the American Association for the New 
York Metropolitans (1883). A center fielder, O'Rourke batted and threw 
left-handed. He was born in Bridgeport, Connecticut. O'Rourke was the older
