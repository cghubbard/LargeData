Sungui Baseball Stadium is a multi-purpose stadium in Incheon, South Korea. 
It is currently used mostly for baseball games. The stadium holds 12,000 
people. The stadium was built in 1934. It was part of Incheon Sungui Sports 
Complex. It was closed in late 2008 and will eventually be replaced by 
a Sungui Arena Park. Coordinates: 37°28′00″N 126°38′34″E﻿ / ﻿37.46667°N
