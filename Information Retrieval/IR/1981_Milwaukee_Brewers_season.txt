The Milwaukee Brewers' 1981 season involved the Brewers' finishing 1st 
in American League East during the second half of the split schedule with 
an overall record of 62 wins and 47 losses. They proceeded to lose to the 
New York Yankees in the ALDS. Rollie Fingers became the first relief pitcher 
in the history of the American League to win the MVP Award.[1] [1] [2]
