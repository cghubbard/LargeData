Chiba) is a Japanese professional baseball player with the Yomiuri Giants 
in Japan's Nippon Professional Baseball. He has played for the Japanese 
national team since 1999 as a catcher. In the 2009 Japan Series, he was 
named the Most Valuable Player. He was the captain of Japanese national
