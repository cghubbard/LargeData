The 1947 New York Giants season was the franchise's 65th season. The team 
finished in fourth place in the National League with an 81-73 record, 13 
games behind the Brooklyn Dodgers. Between September 5 and 23, the Giants 
hit at least one home run in each of 19 games, the longest such streak 
in franchise history (considering records from 1914 onwards).[5] [1] [2]
