John Stephen "Thumper" DeMerit (January 8, 1936 in West Bend, Wisconsin) 
is a former professional baseball player. He was an outfielder over parts 
of five seasons (1957–1959, 1961–1962) with the Milwaukee Braves and New 
York Mets. DeMerit was a member of the 1957 World Series champion Braves. 
He also appeared in 14 games for the 1962 Mets, whose 120 losses continue
