The 1981 Detroit Tigers finished in fourth place in the American League 
East with a record of 31-26 (.544) in the first half of the season, and 
in third place with a record of 29-23 (.558) in the second half, for an 
overall record of 60-49. They outscored their opponents 427 to 404. The 
Tigers drew 1,149,144 fans to Tiger Stadium in 1981, ranking 5th of the
