League title by one game. The Dodgers lost the World Series to the New 
York Yankees in five games. Jackie Robinson led the NL in hitting and stolen 
bases and won the National League Most Valuable Player Award. Robinson 
was the first black player to win the NL MVP.[4] [1] [2] [3] [4] [5] [6]
