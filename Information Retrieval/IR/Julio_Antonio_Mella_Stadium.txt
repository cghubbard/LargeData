Julio Antonio Mella Stadium is a multi-use stadium in Las Tunas, Cuba. 
It is currently used mostly for baseball games and is the home stadium 
of Las Tunas Magos. This team is famous for beating teams invited to Las 
Tunas city. The stadium holds 13,000 people and is the most important in
