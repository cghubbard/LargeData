The 1936 New York Giants season was the franchise's 54th season. The Giants 
won the National League pennant. The team went on to lose to the New York 
Yankees in the 1936 World Series, four games to two. Carl Hubbell became 
the first player to win two National League MVP Awards.[1] [1] [2] [3]
