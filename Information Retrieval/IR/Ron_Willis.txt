Ronald Earl Willis (July 12, 1943 – November 21, 1977) is a former professional 
baseball player. He was a pitcher over parts of 5 seasons (1966–1970) with 
the St. Louis Cardinals, Houston Astros and San Diego Padres. Willis was 
a member of the 1967 World Series champion Cardinals. For his career he 
compiled an 11–12 record with a 3.32 earned run average and 128 strikeouts
