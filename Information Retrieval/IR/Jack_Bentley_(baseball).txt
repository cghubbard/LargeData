John Needles Bentley (March 8, 1895 – October 24, 1969) was a professional 
baseball player. He was a left-handed pitcher over parts of nine seasons 
(1913–1916, 1923–1927) with the Washington Senators, New York Giants and 
Philadelphia Phillies. For his career, he compiled a 46–33 record in 138 
appearances, with a 4.01 earned run average and 259 strikeouts. Bentley 
was a member of the Giants' pennant-winning teams in 1923 and 1924. He
