Gary Robert Lavelle (born January 3, 1949) is a retired professional baseball 
pitcher who played in Major League Baseball from 1974 to 1985 and 1987. 
Lavelle grew up in Pennsylvania, where he attended Liberty High School 
in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania. Lavelle spent the majority of his Major League 
career with the San Francisco Giants, and also played for the Toronto Blue 
Jays and Oakland Athletics before retiring in 1987. He posted a career 
high 13 wins in 1978 and accumulated 136 saves in his 13 season career.
