is a former professional baseball player. He was an outfielder over parts 
of six seasons (1960 - '65) with the St. Louis Cardinals and the Cincinnati 
Reds. James was a member of the Cardinals when they won the World Series 
in 1964. During his Major League baseball career of 510 games, James batted
