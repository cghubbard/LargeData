The Dominican Summer League is a branch of affiliated Minor League Baseball 
which is played in the Dominican Republic. The league was founded in 1985. 
The 2016 72-game season starts June 4 and ends August 27 with the playoffs 
starting on August 29.[1] With closure of the Venezuelan Summer League,[2] 
the league expanded to a record high of 42 teams in six divisions.[3] Each 
team is affiliated with a different major league franchise with twelve 
organizations fielding two teams: the Astros, Cubs, Diamondbacks, Dodgers, 
Mets, Orioles, Phillies, Rangers, Rays, Reds, Red Sox and Yankees. The
