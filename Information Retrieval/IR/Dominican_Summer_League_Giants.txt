The Dominican Summer League Giants or DSL Giants are a minor league baseball 
team in the Dominican Summer League. The team plays in the Boca Chica South 
division and is affiliated with the San Francisco Giants. The team first 
came into existence in 1989, as one of the DSL's original American minor 
league teams. They shared an affiliation with the Detroit Tigers and were 
known as the DSL Tigers/Giants.[1] They were independently affiliated with 
the Giants from 1990 to 1992, before gaining an affiliation with the Philadelphia 
Phillies and the Houston Astros in 1993.[2] For 1994, they shared an affiliation 
with the Baltimore Orioles.[3] The team became independently affiliated 
with the Giants again in 1995 and have been so ever since.[4] Pitchers
