The Canada A's are a future independent professional baseball team representing 
Canada (and Cathedral City, California) that will be based in Palm Springs, 
California as a part of the new California Winter League. They will play 
their games in a short-season schedule from January to February at Palm 
Springs Stadium along with the Palm Springs Chill, Coachella Valley Snowbirds 
and Palm Desert Coyotes. They started as Team Canada in the rival Arizona 
Winter League, a short-season instructional winter league affiliated with 
the Golden Baseball League and played in the International Division. But
