Ambrose J. McGann (1868 - February 2, 1941) was an infielder/outfielder 
in Major League Baseball, playing mainly at shortstop for the Louisville 
Colonels during the 1895 season. He was born in Baltimore, Maryland. Batting 
side and throwing arm are unknown. In one season career, McGann was a .288 
hitter (21-for-73) with nine runs and nine RBI in 20 games, including five 
doubles, two triples, six stolen bases, and a .358 on-base percentage.
