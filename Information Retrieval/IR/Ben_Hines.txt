Benjamin Thortan Hines (born November 7, 1935 in Yeager, Oklahoma) is a 
former coach in Major League Baseball. Hines is best remembered as the 
batting coach for the Los Angeles Dodgers, a position he held from 1985-1986 
and 1988-1993. Hines played infield and catcher in the Baltimore Orioles 
chain in 1960-1961. He then became manager of the University of La Verne 
team until 1980 and won NAIA coach of the year honors in 1977. He managed 
the independent league team Alaska Goldpanners from 1978-1982. One of the
