Neal Heaton (born March 3, 1960 in Jamaica, New York) is a former Major 
League Baseball left-handed pitcher who played for the Cleveland Indians, 
Minnesota Twins, Montreal Expos, Pittsburgh Pirates, Kansas City Royals, 
Milwaukee Brewers, and New York Yankees from 1982 to 1993. Heaton was drafted 
by the Indians in the 2nd round of the 1981 amateur draft from the University 
of Miami. He was selected to the National League All-Star team in 1990 
with the Pirates. In his 12-season career, he posted an 80-96 record with
