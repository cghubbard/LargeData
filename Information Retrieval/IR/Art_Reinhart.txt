Arthur Conrad Reinhart (May 29, 1899 - November 11, 1946) was a Major League 
Baseball pitcher from 1919-1928. Reinhart was in born in Ackley, Iowa, 
and attended the University of Iowa. He was a left-handed pitcher and pitched 
in the Texas League and other minor leagues.[1] Reinhart pitched for the 
St. Louis Cardinals from 1919 to 1928. He played in a total of 123 games, 
45 as a starting pitcher. He had a record of 30 wins and 18 losses.[2]
