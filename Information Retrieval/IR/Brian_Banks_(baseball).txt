Brian Glen Banks (born September 28, 1970), is a former Major League Baseball 
player from Mountain View High School who played first base, outfield, 
and catcher in the Major Leagues from 1996-2003. He played for the Florida 
Marlins and Milwaukee Brewers. Banks was a part of the 2003 World Series 
Champion Florida Marlins. He retired from professional baseball on August 
20, 2004. Following his baseball career, Brian returned to school and graduated 
in 2011 from the Arizona School of Dentistry and Oral Health. Dr. Banks
