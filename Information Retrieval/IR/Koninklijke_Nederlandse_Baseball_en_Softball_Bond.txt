The Koninklijke Nederlandse Baseball en Softball Bond (Royal Netherlands 
Baseball and Softball Federation)[1] is the national governing body of 
baseball in the Netherlands. Baseball is played in all the provinces, but 
it struggles for popularity due to the popularity of many other sports, 
including the sport of soccer, in which the Netherlands has always been 
one of the leading nations. School-aged children are generally guided toward 
a future in soccer, rather than baseball. Since 1922, the national major
