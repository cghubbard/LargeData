The Philadelphia White Stockings were an early professional baseball team. 
They were a member of the National Association from 1873 to 1875. Their 
home games were played at the Jefferson Street Grounds. They were managed 
by Fergy Malone, Jimmy Wood, Bill Craver, Mike McGeary, and Bob Addy. During 
their three-year existence the White Stockings won 102 games and lost 77
