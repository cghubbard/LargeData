The Dominican Summer Pirates are a minor league baseball team in the Dominican 
Summer League. The team, also known as the DSL Pirates, plays in the Boca 
Chica, Dominican Republic, as a member of the Boca Chica North Division 
and are affiliated with the Pittsburgh Pirates. Past players include Aramis 
Ramírez, José Guillén and Ronny Paulino. The club was known as the Dominican 
Summer League Pirates 1, or DSL Pirates 1, between 2012 and 2013, when 
the Venezuelan Summer Pirates were relocated to the Dominican Republic 
to become the Dominican Summer League Pirates 2.[1] In 2014, the Pirates 
returned to fielding one DSL team, and the team became known once again
