The Dominican Summer League Reds are a minor league baseball team of the 
Dominican Summer League. They are located in Boca Chica, Santo Domingo, 
Dominican Republic, and play their home games at Ciudad de Baseball. The 
team plays in the Boca Chica Baseball City division and is affiliated with
