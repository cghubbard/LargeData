The New York Yankees' 2002 season was the 100th season for the Yankees 
in New York, and their 102nd overall going back to their origins in Baltimore. 
The team finished with a record of 103-58 finishing 10.5 games ahead of 
the Boston Red Sox. New York was managed by Joe Torre. The Yankees played 
at Yankee Stadium. In the playoffs, they lost in the ALDS in 4 games to
