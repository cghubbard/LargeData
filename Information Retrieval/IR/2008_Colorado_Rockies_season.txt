The Colorado Rockies' 2008 season was the team's 16th season overall. The 
Rockies attempted to repeat their previous season's success after making 
it to the World Series; however, they ended up finishing third place in 
the National League West with a record of 74-88. The Rockies drew 2,650,218 
fans for the season, their highest total since 2002. The average home attendance
